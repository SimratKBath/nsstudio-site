The questions that come up most.

The honest answers.

What do I do with the strategy once you leave?

The deliverable isn't the strategy document — it's the decisions. You leave the engagement with explicit kill criteria, a decision-rights map, vendor evaluation frameworks, and the audit cadence to know whether your build team is on the right track. We don't manage your sprint. We do tell you when your sprint is going in the wrong direction.

Can't I just build the AI strategy myself with ChatGPT?

ChatGPT generates frameworks. It does not have a seat in the meeting where six executives each have a valid claim on AI accountability and nobody can stop a failing pilot. What we deliver is clarity on specific decision points — before the contract, before the pilot, before the board asks why the numbers haven't moved. No model generates that clarity. Someone who has seen the failure patterns does.

If you don't build it, how do you know if it's buildable?

We assess feasibility in terms of decision logic, ownership, data readiness, and vendor selection — not model architecture or infrastructure. We will tell you whether the thing you're proposing can be governed and measured. For "can it be trained on our data," you need a different kind of help — we'll tell you what that help should cost and how to evaluate it.

How is NS Studio different from a normal consulting firm?

Three things, all structural. First, every engagement is run by a senior founder — no juniors on client work. Second, every engagement is productized: fixed scope, fixed price, fixed deliverables. We don't sell hourly time or open-ended retainers. Third, we take a maximum of three engagements per quarter. That last one is a real constraint, not a marketing line — it's why clients get the level of attention boutique work is supposed to deliver.

What's the typical engagement length?

AI Audit is a two-week sprint. Strategy Sprint runs four to six weeks depending on scope. Embedded Advisor is a three-month minimum retainer. Every engagement starts with a one-week diagnostic — if the work isn't paying for itself by then, we end the engagement and refund the deposit.

Do you actually build the AI products, or just advise?

We design the spec, pick the tools, and supervise the build. We do not write production code. For implementation, we either partner with your engineering team or recommend a build partner we've worked with before. The studio stays focused on strategy, design, and decision-making — that's where the leverage is.

What industries do you work with?

We focus on three: education and EdTech (our flagship case study and area of strongest pattern recognition), mid-market B2B SaaS and operations (50–500 employees), and seed–Series A startups where AI is a core part of the product thesis. We've also done work with public-sector and nonprofit organizations, particularly where our California Small Business Certification (ID 2045105) opens up set-aside contract paths.

How do you measure success?

Every engagement has explicit, written success criteria signed off before kickoff. For Pilot Triage, success is a defended kill list and at least one decision worth more than the engagement fee. For Strategy Sprint, success is three workflows redesigned and a measurement plan with before/after metrics running in production. For Embedded Advisor, success is monthly briefings the CEO can act on and zero AI compliance fires.

Will I work directly with the founder?

Yes. Every engagement. That's the entire reason the studio exists at this size. If you're talking to anyone other than the founder during an engagement, something's gone wrong.

What happens after the engagement ends?

Pilot Triage includes 30 days of email/Slack follow-up. Strategy Sprint includes 60 days. Embedded Advisor is ongoing by definition. We don't lock clients into surprise renewals — every engagement ends cleanly with a written handoff document and a list of recommended next steps, which you can take to another partner or do yourselves.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, before any substantive conversation about your business. Send yours over or use ours — both are fine.

How does the California Small Business Certification work?

NS Studio LLC is certified as a California Small Business (DGS ID 2045105). For California state agencies, municipalities, and qualifying public-sector buyers, this means we can be contracted under set-aside, preference, or simplified procurement programs depending on the agency. If you're in public-sector procurement, mention this on the discovery call and we'll walk through the eligibility specifics.

What if my AI question doesn't fit cleanly into one of your engagement types?

Tell us anyway. The three engagement types cover most cases, but the diagnostic call costs you nothing and gives us enough to recommend honestly — whether that's a custom scope, a referral to someone better suited, or a recommendation to wait six months until the question is clearer.

Is there a free option?

Yes — the 5 Fatal Flaws guide is a 12-minute PDF in the same voice and method we use in paid engagements. Read it, decide if our thinking matches yours, then book the call (or don't). The guide is also a useful pre-read before any engagement — about 60% of clients arrive having already read it.

How fast can we start?

If a slot is open, kickoff is within two weeks of contract. If no slot is open, we hold a waitlist and tell you honestly when the next opening is. We don't take engagements we can't staff at the founder level — that's the whole point.
