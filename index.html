The method

The 4-step engagement. Same shape every time, different answers.

We adapted the frameworks Paul Graham uses to evaluate startups , for AI product decisions inside companies. Brutally honest. Specific. Always actionable.

  1. 1

    Pressure-test the assumption.

    Every AI initiative rests on one core assumption. We find it, name it, and stress-test it before a single dollar more is spent. Most assumptions don't survive this step, and that's the point.

  2. 2

    Map the real competitive landscape.

    Not the obvious competitors. The behaviors and tools your users currently rely on, including spreadsheets, ChatGPT, and "we just deal with it." Differentiation lives here.

  3. 3

    Pick the most profitable product.

    Of the candidates, which one has the highest ROI per week shipped? We rank explicitly and tell you which to kill. Decision rights stay with you. Conviction stays with us.

  4. 4

    Ship it: or hand it off cleanly.

    A 90-day plan with weekly milestones. Embedded advisor option if you need a senior partner in the room. Governance guardrails that actually fit how your team works.
